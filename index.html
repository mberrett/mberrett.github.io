Hi!

I'm currently a Data Scientist at Healthfirst in the department of Enterprise Analytics. Before becoming a practicing Data Scientist in the healthcare industry, I spent several years in academia, where I focused on clinical research, developing novel methods to predict chronic disease outcomes. These forecasts, in turn, help providers pick the right treatment for their patients, whether it be long term disease management or aggressive intervention. In particular, I'm interested in time-aware modeling, deep learning, and the combination of the two (see Master's Thesis).

Data Scientist at Healthfirst.

In New York City 1 out of every 7 people you see on the subway are on a HealthFirst plan.

With 1.4 million members, Healthfirst is the biggest non-profit Managed Care Organization in the Downstate NY area, offering a variety of government-sponsored healthcare plans such as Medicaid and Medicare.

As a Data Scientist at Healthfirst, my first big achievement was bringing Medicare Churn Modeling in-house, beating third party vendors across the board in terms of scope and accuracy for only a fraction of the price. Quickly after, I acted as Data Science evangelist, presenting decks on Deep Learning to various C-suite level executives, successfuly lobbying for the Data Science Team's increased priviliges and access to new technologies.

I'm a leader in technologically innovation, helping Healthfirst's Data Science team — and Enterprise Analytics Department at large — go far above and beyond its competition. I single-handedly lead the adoption of Github (for version control and project documentation), and Spark (for previously unfeasible big data tasks). I was also instrumental in the adoption of Amazon services such as EC2 (for remote and superior processing power) and S3 (for remote and superior storage capabilities). I've consistently worked to optimize machine learning workflows, focusing, in particular, on feature engineering; readbility, reproducibility and resiliency are my guiding principles.

Best of all, I've achieved all of these things while mantaining strict adherence to HIPAA's Security Rules, always mindful of Protected Healh Information (PHI).

This outstanding performance at Healthfirst has gotten me a fair amount of attention, so that in the span of only 14 months, my salary has increased by 20%.

Sharing is power. I firmly believe in an abundance mentality. I am commited to being a life-long learner.

I will continue fine-tuning health related predictions, optimizing workflows, and sharing knowledge with my colleagues at Healthfirst and beyond.
